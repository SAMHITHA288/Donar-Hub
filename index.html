Earthquakes

Earthquakes strike without warning, causing widespread devastation, loss of lives, and displacement. Survivors urgently need shelter, medical aid, food, and clean water. Your support can help rebuild communities, provide relief, and restore hope.

Tsunami

Tsunamis are powerful ocean waves triggered by underwater earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, or landslides. They can cause massive destruction, sweeping away homes, flooding cities, and leaving thousands displaced. Survivors urgently need food, clean water, medical care, and shelter. Your support can help rebuild lives and provide immediate relief to affected communities..

Floods

Floods are among the most devastating natural disasters, causing widespread destruction and loss of life. They occur when water overflows onto normally dry land due to excessive rainfall, storm surges, or dam failures. Understanding the causes, effects, and safety measures can help mitigate their impact.

Landslides

A landslide is the movement of rock, soil, and debris down a slope due to gravity. It can occur suddenly or develop over time, causing significant damage to landscapes, infrastructure, and human life. Landslides are often triggered by natural and human-induced factors, making them a common geological hazard worldwide..
